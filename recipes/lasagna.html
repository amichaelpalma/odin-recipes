The Miracle Lasagna

Description

A traditional, full-flavored lasagna with three cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar Prego Traditional Italian Sauce
  • 6 each uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Spread about 1 cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish (11x7-inch).
    Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese and 1 cup pasta sauce.
    Top with remaining 3 uncooked noodles and remaining pasta sauce. Cover

  2. Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour.
    Uncover the top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
    Let stand for 5 minutes.
